About Me

My Journey & Background

Hello! I'm Albin Shibu, a recent Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) graduate from St. Mary's College, Sulthan Bathery, which is affiliated with the University of Calicut. My academic journey has provided me with a strong foundation in computer applications, programming paradigms, and database designs.

During my academic years, I realized that computer logic and visual storytelling share a lot in common—both require clean structure, precision framing, and detailed rendering. I began combining my BCA computing foundation with videography and video editing. Using tools like CapCut, I focus on executing high-tempo visual cuts, smooth keyframe curves, color grading, and dynamic designs that elevate digital content.
